About Us


The University of Belagavi (known earlier as University of Belgaum) is one of the oldest and premier Universities in India. It was established in 1969 consequent upon "Wood's Education Dispatch", and it is one amongst the first three Universities in India. As a sequel to the change in the name of the city from Belgaum to Belagavi, the name of the University has been changed from "University of Belgaum" to "University of Belagavi", vide notification issued by the Government of karnataka and published in the Government Gazette dated 4th September, 1996. The University was accorded 5 star status in 2001 & 'A' grade status in April 2012 by the National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC). It has been granted University with Potential for Excellence (UPE) status by UGC and PURSE Scheme by DST.
